This form, adopted from NYSERDA, assists emerging and growing companies in determining the technical and commercial maturity of their products or innovations. It utilizes a customized and integrated tool that measures both the Technology Readiness Level (TRL) and Commercial Readiness Level (CRL). This TRL/CRL tool is based on systems developed by NASA, DOE, and ARPA-E.
